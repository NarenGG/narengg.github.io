About

I’m currently a high school senior at the Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science, with interests bridging computational photonics, law & society, and theory. My research spans non-equilibrium heat transfer methods for hearing preservation. I'm also a nationally-ranked LD debater focused on critical legal and literary theory.

Projects & Research

  • 1D Finite-Difference Time-Domain (FDTD) simulation of thermal currents
  • 2D/3D COMSOL simulations of emissivity via Green's function approach
  • Community outreach & research through TAMS RO

Debate

Lincoln-Douglas debater focused on international relations, structural injustice, and continental philosophy. Quaterfinalist and 3rd speaker at WKU Spring Showcase and NFA-LD Nationals qualification, with a focus on kritikal debate.
